Brendan Anning

Wedding Celebrant

The perfect balance of enjoyable meaningful wedding ceremonies.

Hi, my name is Brendan. I love helping amazing humans create awesome wedding ceremonies where everyone feels relaxed and the full of good vibes. With over 10 years experience I have found the perfect balance between fun yet meaningful ceremonies.

I was born on new years eve, therefore, I always say I was born to party! A wedding ceremony is a massive celebration and sets the vibe for the rest of your day.

I live in Brisbane, and like to go out of my way to make the process of planning your ceremony easy and exciting. We can Skype or meet in person to see if we can create a ceremony that you and all your favourite people will love.

My service speaks for itself.
